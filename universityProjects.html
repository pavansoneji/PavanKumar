NEON TAG

Neon Tag is a FPS (First person shooter) Multiplayer, Action Sport Game. That focuses on combat Multiplayer Gameplay in a virtual digital world experience.
This is a Sci-fi First person shooter, multiplayer game, it take characteristics from first person shooter games, and real life laser tag game.

Mechanics:

  • Health Box.
  • Floating platforms.
  • Mirror walls.
  • Walk, sprint.
  • Jump.
  • Shoot projectiles.

Concept:

Neon Tag is a FPS (First person shooter) Multiplayer, Action Sport Game. That focuses on combat Multiplayer Gameplay in a virtual digital world experience. Where player tries to tag other players in the arena, the last player stands win the game, in an action fast-Paced gameplay, with a high speed futuristic neon powered guns. In order to win the game, the player must stay the last online(alive) player. This game is inspired by the real life Laser tag game.


SPACE RUNNER

The player goal is to survive, make the high score, shoot enemies and save himself from enemy's projectiles and obstacles.
The space runner is my 5th-semester group project at LIMKOKWING University. This game is an endless side scroll game made by UE4 using blueprints I did all the programmatic work in this game.

Mechanics:

  • Auto forward movement.
  • Multiple jumps.
  • Enemy generate system.
  • Platform generate system.
  • Aim the gun point on the mouse position on some angle.
  • Shoot projectiles.

Enemies list:

  • Robotic Tank: Range attack.
  • Robotic drown: Range attack.

AI Mechanics:

  • Robotic tank moves on the ground and aim the player and shoot a projectile towards the player.
  • Robotic drown fly and follow the player y position and attach the player by using the gun.

LOYAL FIGHT

Loyal Fight is a fighting game. The player has to collect all the keys for opening the boss level door.
The loyal fight is also my 3rd semester at LIMKOKWING University I used the content from the internet in the game because it was a solo project. The game made by using a unity3D game engine.

Mechanics:

  • Walking, running and single jump
  • Kick and punch collisions
  • Hold to special kick move
  • Enemies appear on a certain radius.
  • Player fall away when get hit.

Enemies List:

  • Skull: Melee attack.
  • Dracula: Melee attack.
  • Snow Man: Range attack
  • Evil Ryu: Powerful range attack.

AI Mechanics:

  • Skull enemy move on a platform without falling.
  • Skull enemy turn back if the player is behind.
  • Skull enemy attack when player is near.
  • Dracula enemy perform melee attack when player is near him.
  • Snow man enemy perform a range attach
  • Boss move and jump from one platform to another platform. Perform a powerful range attack.
